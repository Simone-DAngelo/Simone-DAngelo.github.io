About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in aerial robotics at the PRISMA Laboratory, University of Naples Federico II. I received my Ph.D. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems in 2025.

My research focuses on control systems, state estimation, and autonomy for unmanned aerial vehicles, with particular emphasis on aerial manipulation and contact-rich interaction.

During my Ph.D., I was a visiting researcher at Toronto Metropolitan University (Canada) and Technical University of Denmark.

Research Interests

  • Aerial robotics and UAV autonomy
  • Aerial manipulation and physical interaction
  • Model-based and nonlinear control
  • Interaction control and force estimation
  • Trajectory planning and navigation
